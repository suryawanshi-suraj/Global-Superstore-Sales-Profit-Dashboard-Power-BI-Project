# Global-Superstore-Sales-Profit-Dashboard-Power-BI-Project

## 📊 Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive Power BI dashboard analyzing Global Superstore sales and profit data. The dashboard provides actionable insights into sales trends, profit margins, and business performance across different segments, regions, and product categories.

## 🎯 Dashboard Features

### Key Metrics & KPIs
- **Total Sales**: Overall revenue generated
- **Total Profit**: Net profit across all transactions
- **Profit Margin**: Percentage-based profitability analysis
- **Sales Growth**: Year-over-year and period-over-period trends
- **Number of Orders**: Transaction volume and order frequency

### Visual Analytics
- **Sales by Region**: Geographic distribution of sales performance
- **Sales by Segment**: Analysis across Customer, Corporate, and Home Office segments
- **Product Category Performance**: Breakdown of sales and profit by product categories
- **Top Products**: Identification of best-performing products
- **Temporal Trends**: Monthly and yearly sales and profit patterns
- **Profitability Analysis**: Profit margins by category, region, and segment

## 📈 Data Insights
- Identify high-performing and underperforming regions
- Analyze customer segment profitability
- Track seasonal trends and sales patterns
- Monitor product category performance
- Discover opportunities for profit optimization
